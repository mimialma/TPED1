Take a look at me and my design journey!

Hello!

My name is Alma Mimi Arnold, but everyone calls me Mimi. I am 21 years old and I study at the Faculty of Design in Ljubljana. I prefer designing logos and I love typography. I like to incorporate color and texture into my designs. Last year I got excited about calligraphy.
I am currently located in Alicante, Spain, because I am doing the first semester of the third year on an ERASMUS+ exchange at EASDA.

My design journey

Design has been with me since childhood, since my parents are also graphic designers. I started my professional design journey at the High School of Design and a Photography Ljubljana. My Bachelors study program in Visual Communications at the Faculty of Design and focuses on typography, web design, illustration and visual communications planning.
